Learning Plan

25 June 2023

My long-term goal and career pathway

My long-term goal would be to become proficient using the technologies and software skills that I learn at Dev Academy. An Ideal career pathway for me would be to start with an internship and then to secure a permanent full-time position within web development.

My strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

I think my research skills are a valuable strength to have because this experience often requires a lot of self-learning. I have some prior learning experience in software development so I think that contributes to my ability to do efficient research. I have implemented the growth mindset which I think will help with my development in skills and talents as I like to challenge myself to learn more through hard work and dedication. As for limitations I'd say poor time management would be one as sometimes I get so caught up in one thing and forget I should be spending time in something else I should be doing. Another would be procrastination which usually happens when I have to do something I dislike. Luckily, I have been enjoying myself so far but I plan to overcome this limitation.

Skills I would like to see developed while at Dev Academy

I would like to develop my communication skills as I want to become more effective at conveying my thoughts and ideas. I hope to also improve my creativity, as I feel like I could definitely think more outside the box. I plan to improve on this by exploring multiple solutions of creativity and doing experiments of my own.

How I will manage my workload in this programme

How I will manage myself to work productively and safely with other learners, Facilitators and industry/community representatives

I will manage my productivity by setting goals each day as to what tasks I should complete and making sure I finish them before the end of the day. Reflection is a big part of this programme I found that it helps to understand and gain insight in the learning material. I will also do some reflection of my own so that I can remember the key concepts and to identify ways to improve. I plan to improve my productivity by eliminating distractions such as not having my phone nearby and listening to brown noise when it gets a little noisy. I'm also using a separate account for this programme so that I don't get tempted to slack off.

What I will do when things are building up and starting to get to me

If things are starting to get tough then the first thing I would do is to see if I can re-evaluate my goals for the day so that I can maybe work on something easier before getting back to those more difficult goals. Breaking up the large tasks into smaller ones can also help with achieving the goals I set. Taking a break is an important part of remaining productive to relieve stress and gather my thoughts. I notice that it can also lead to solutions as I find myself often coming up with ideas when I'm lying in bed or when I'm in the middle of doing something else.

How and where I will seek help in a timely way

I usually look to online research when I'm stuck but if I cannot find the answer that I am looking for within a reasonable amount of time then I will seek guidance from the facilitators or peers in the discord channel.

What I expect from the Facilitation team

A positive learning environment is important for everyone taking part because it has an impact on our engagement which I think is crucial for our learning experience. The main thing I expect is a response within a timely manner to clarify any questions that may be asked and clear explanations on the learning material.
